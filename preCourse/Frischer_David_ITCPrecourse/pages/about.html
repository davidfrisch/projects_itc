About me

Why I like coding ?

The reason I like coding is because it is the right balance for me between play and work. I hope that one day, through the programs I make, society can become better.

My personal experience with coding

I started coding at the age of 18. Until today, I have written programs in these different languages:

  • Java
  • C
  • Haskell
  • Erlang
  • Python

Since a few days, I started to learn Javscript to become a Full Stack programmer!

My education

Ecole Nouvelle de la Suisse Romande

From the age of 12 to 18, I did my compulsory schooling at the Ecole Nouvelle de la Suisse Romande. It was a medium-sized school where I met some good friends with whom I am still in contact today.

University of Fribourg

I did my Bachelor in Computer Science at the University of Fribourg for 3 years. I took many courses such as: Design Patterns, Introduction to Databases, Algorithms and even a Machine Learning course.
